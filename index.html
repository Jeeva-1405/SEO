M.S. DHONI

Technical Analysis: The Finishing Engine

Role: Wicket-keeper Batsman / Tactical Advisor

Auction price: (Retained/Mentor)

Born: July 7, 1981, Ranchi, Bihar

Dhoni's value is derived from his **match intelligence (IQ)** and elite **Death Overs Strike Rate (SR)**, particularly against pace. His core technical skill is his ability to maintain a **Still Head** while executing the helicopter shot or the flat-bat jab, generating power from rapid hip rotation rather than backlift.

Tactical & Statistical Breakdown

CSK utilizes him as a **Specialist Finisher (Overs 17-20)**. His average SR in these overs consistently exceeds **200**, primarily exploiting the small boundaries at Chepauk. His key technical vulnerability remains slower bowlers or off-spinners using a wider line outside the off-stump in the middle phase.
